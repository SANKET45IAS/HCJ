India is a country in South Asia, known for its rich history, cultural diversity, and vibrant traditions. With over 1.4 billion people, it is the world's second most populous country. New Delhi is its capital, while Mumbai is the financial hub. India is famous for its iconic monuments like the Taj Mahal and the Qutub Minar. The nation’s economy is growing rapidly, with a focus on technology and innovation. Despite its progress, India still faces challenges such as poverty and inequality, but efforts to uplift its citizens continue. Known for its spiritual heritage, India is home to many religions, including Hinduism, Buddhism, and Sikhism.
